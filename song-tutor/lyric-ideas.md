# Lyric Ideas Sketchpad

A workspace for capturing song lyric ideas, themes, and phrases that can be developed into full songs.

---

## Format Guide

Use this structure for each lyric idea:

### Entry Template

```
## [Song Title / Working Title]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Mood/Genre:** [e.g., melancholic indie-folk, upbeat pop, blues]  
**Inspiration:** [What sparked this idea? A moment, emotion, image, etc.]

### Core Theme
[One sentence summary of what the song is about]

### Key Phrases / Hook Ideas
- [Memorable phrase or potential chorus line]
- [Another key lyric or hook]
- [Potential opening/closing line]

### Verse Sketches
```
[Raw lyric lines, even if incomplete]
[Don't worry about rhyme scheme yet]
[Just capture the emotional core]
```

### Chorus / Refrain Ideas
```
[Potential chorus structure]
[What's the emotional payoff?]
```

### Inspiration from Other Songs
- **Song Title / Artist:** [What specifically caught you? Lyrical device, hook, structure, emotional delivery, etc.]
- **How I want to use this:** [Adapt the technique? Explore a similar theme differently? Match the energy?]

### Notes & Development Ideas
- [Rhyme scheme thoughts]
- [Rhythm or meter notes]
- [Questions to explore in this song]
- [Areas that need work]

### Status
- [ ] Early sketch
- [ ] Developing
- [ ] Ready for melody
- [ ] Complete draft
- [ ] Refined

---
```

## How I'll Help

When you share an entry, I can:
- **Suggest rhyme schemes** and lyrical improvements
- **Identify themes** and emotional arcs
- **Offer alternative phrasings** that fit your mood
- **Spot patterns** in your writing style
- **Develop hooks** into fuller choruses
- **Challenge weak lines** and suggest stronger alternatives
- **Help with meter/rhythm** if you have a melody in mind
