**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Mood / Genre:** (e.g., melancholic folk, upbeat pop, blues)  
**Initial Inspiration:** (What sparked this idea?)

**Core Theme:**  
One or two sentences about what this song is really about.

**Key Phrases:**
- Hook or memorable line idea
- Another compelling phrase
- Potential chorus kernel

**Verse / Chorus Sketches:**
Raw, unpolished lyric fragments and ideas. Don't worry about perfection here — just capture the feeling and direction.

**Inspiration from Other Songs:**
- **Song:** [Title] by [Artist]

- Rhythm / meter thoughts

- Emotional arc notes
